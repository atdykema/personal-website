My name is Andrew Dykema.

To give my story a little context, I’m a 21 year-old student of UMass Amherst student who was born and raised about 40 minutes outside of Boston in Holliston, MA.

I’ve always created, explored, and questioned, only the mediums in which I achieve these behaviors are the only things which have changed since then. From wandering in the woods as a Boy Scout and creating games using Scratch to designing my own single blockchain mining node in Python and configuring the deployment of websites using Terraform and Docker.

This love of discovery and creativity is what has driven me to dive into the many ambitious endeavors and casual hobbies.

My discovery of the blockchain and its many applications opened my eyes to the intricatracy and awe-inspiring of trustless and decentralized networks.

My passion for programming and code allows me to create and articulate any idea in a fashion which can be universally understood and built upon.

My adventures in the world of video editing lends itself well to expressing my spontaneously absurd and abstract creative desire in an impactful visual and auditory piece of media.

My appreciation of music helps to aid my perspective in understanding how boundless and intangible the world of creative expression can be.

I am a culmination of the desire to learn and experience, and what I have achieved and accomplished thus far is a product of those desires. Because of my passions, I cannot wait to continue to explore and create as I progress through my life and I look forward to seeing what the future holds!
